About


The Inner Workings


SCNX uses your location to categorize your photos while you're on the go.
SCNX will create an album when you arrive at a new location based on the city.
You can also set it all manual and created albums with very small and specific or very large regions to track.

SCNX finds all the albums in range, save the photo to them and applies your chosen filters.


Filters


Setting one filter for an entire series is like using a film camera that has one distinctive look for the entire roll. It is no longer an effect, it becomes a style.

SCNX comes with 20 filters and future filters will always be free.


Camera Controls


SCNX works like any other fully automatic camera app right out of the box.
For more control you can adjust the exposure and set the focus right where you want it.

Privacy

SCNX doesn't call home.
Location data, usage data and photographs will never be uploaded to a service we or a third party has access to. Sharing photographs is possible through the build in Photo App or Facebook, sharing is entirely up to the user.
